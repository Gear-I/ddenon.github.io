About

Cybersecurity professional with experience spanning Security Operations (SOC), Digital Forensics & Incident Response (DFIR), threat intelligence, identity and access management, and application security. My work combines operational security experience, open-source contributions, and forensic research to investigate, validate, and understand digital evidence.

I contribute to the LEAPPs Digital Forensics Platform, develop DFIR-focused open-source projects, and maintain hands-on experience through homelabs, threat hunting exercises, and forensic investigations. My areas of interest include Windows forensics, mobile forensics, artifact correlation, SIEM technologies, and incident response.

"Follow the timeline. Validate the artifacts. Let the evidence tell the story".

Opinions are mine only, subject to change, and does not represent anyone else.

Contributor to the LEAPPs Digital Forensics Python Platform — open-source tools for iOS and Android forensics. Research notes at ddenoon.blogspot.com and Medium. All certifications on Credly.
